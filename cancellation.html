Cancellation Policy

Cancellation and Change of Booking

  • 1 The Distance Selling Regulations do not provide for cancellation of a contract for leisure services. Accordingly, you do not have a right to cancel this contract. Notwithstanding the Distance Selling Regulations, We, will at our discretion, allow cancellation of your Booking in the following circumstances and You will pay the following amounts.
    • 1.1 Bookings cancelled by you within 7 days of the Booking date will receive no refund and You will pay in full the amount of the Charge contained in Your Booking unless it is a Bank Holiday or Bank Holiday weekend (see 6.1.3 below for Bank Holiday cancellation charges).
    • 1.2 For Bookings cancelled by you more than 7 days of the Booking Date You will be fully refunded by us excluding the handling fee of 3.5% in order to pay for handling from our provider. (see below for Bank Holiday cancellation charges).
    • 1.3 For Bookings on a Bank holiday or Bank Holiday weekend that are cancelled by you within 14 days of the Booking date no refund will be given. For Bookings on a Bank holiday or Bank Holiday weekend that are cancelled by you more than 30 days of the Booking date will pay 20% of the full amount of the charge contained in Your booking.
  • 2 We reserve the right to cancel or curtail a booking or exclude individuals from a party who fail to meet the conditions of section 5 above; or when, for whatever reason, having considered the condition or behaviour of a person it is not in our opinion safe to do otherwise.
  • 3 In the event that You wish to bring a Booking forward We will make no Charge to change the Booking (subject to their being availability to bring Your Booking forward). Charges will be applied to the Booking as those applicable at the time of the change to your Booking as if it were a new Booking. You will pay any additional Charges that may apply following that change. We will refund to You any saving in Charges that may also arise from any changes. Any changes are subject to Our acceptance and we reserve the right not to accept any change.
  • 4 If You wish to postpone your Booking this will be done out of our discretion, subject to their being availability to postpone Your Booking to your requested date. Charges will be applied to the Booking as those applicable at the time of the change to your Booking as if it were a new Booking. You will pay any additional Charges that may apply following that change. We will refund to You any saving in Charges that may also arise from any change. Any changes are subject to Our acceptance, and we reserve the right not to accept any change.
  • 5 In the event that You fail to present yourself at the required time for the fulfilment of your Booking We will treat your Booking as cancelled and your booking will be refunded directly to the credit or debit card that was used to purchase the package(s).
  • 6 In the event that we have to cancel your booking due to the weather conditions being unsuitable, You will be entitled to move Your booking to a future date (subject to availability) or put your booking on hold.
  • 7 COVID-19, During times of Government restrictions, all bookings can be amended up to the 24 hours before your trip. So, if participants are unable to attend you can re-schedule your booking to a later date or put your booking on hold.
  • 8 This section does not affect your rights in the event that you have a genuine and valid complaint about the way We have provided Services to you.
