Who We Are

Once this business was the idea of a young beekeeper. Now, it's his job!

"I always wanted to be self-employeed and have a job that I absolutely love, and now I actually have both!

Beekeeping is such an exciting and inspirational hobby - I cannot describe how important honeybees are for the world and how incredible the different cycles of they have are. It's just phenomenal and I am happy to be(e) sharing what I've learnt with people"

Hour session

Our 60 minute session is a short but great introduction to Beekeeping and the workings of a beehive. Turn up, debrief & suit up! After a brief intro to what equipment you will need, get straight on with handling the bees yourself and learning about what you see! Suitable for 1-2 people.

Two Hour session

Enjoy our 120 minute package, which will include an introduction, briefing and practical experience in the hives. Designed as a more "meaty" and detailed experience, you will be shown the different tools and equipment, what to look for in a bee inspection, and how to manage your own bees. After, the Beekeeper will be available for question and answer discussions with those who want to take up the hobby themselves, or perhaps just want to plant better for the bees! If necessary, a short break with tea and coffee will be provided. Suitable for 1-3 people.

Family Session

This 60 minute package is ideal for a group of up to 4 people! Children 16 years of age or under go FREE! This fun activity is great for both parents and children, as the exciting world of Beekeeping is brought right to their fingertips. The session includes, an introduction to equipment, the life cycle of honeybees, how to support bees at home, and of course the practical experience inside the hive! You'll be guided through it and taught about what you're seeing each step of the way, from Eggs, to the Queen, to Honey!

IMPORTANT!

All experiences ARE WEATHER DEPENDANT! If the weather takes a turn for the worst on your selecte day, we will either re-schedule or refund you.

FAQs

When does booking open/close?

The season for experiences beings from mid-April and ends in mid-September - weather dependant.

Are the sessions weather dependant?

YES! Please be sure to check your emails on the day of your booking, as we ,ay have to contact you to cancel your appointment due to poor weather - we cannot open hives when it's too cold or raining! We will re-schedule, or may have to refund you.

What's the age limit?

All ages are welcome, but we recommend that chuldren under 5 years of age should not be handling the bees. Remember thought, under 16s go FREE with our Family Package.

What should I bring on the day?

Please makes sure to bring with you suitable footwear for potentially muddy terrain - wellies are advised! Please make sure you have sufficient, suitable clothing for the weather on the day - DO NOT bring brightly coloured clothing.

Will I get stung?

We have to take precaution and warn you that you may well get stung on these experiences. We will ask you to sign a disclaimer on the day you arrive to warn you that we do not accept the liability of injury in the unlikely event of you getting stung for any reason. Any questions, please feel free to ring us!
